A Maker of Good Times

Tucked into the heart of downtown Youngsville, Burnt Barrel is the kind of place you show up for one drink and stay for the whole evening. A cozy, hip beer bar with big heart — we're all about friends, music, and discovering your next favorite pour.

Whether you're pulling up a barstool with old friends, discovering a new craft beer, or catching a live set out on the patio, this is your home away from home on Main Street.

Outside food is always welcome — because great drinks deserve great company (and food trucks out front don't hurt either).
